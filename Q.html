common questions



Should I go to the gym ?

Going to the gym to exercise is not necessary at all, but it can be practiced even indoors; It is possible to practice running or walking outside the house, and resistance exercises inside it simply. It is easier than many think, as the exercise does not require a specific place and time.





How do I start exercises ?

- Start slowly You should start exercising slowly and not overdo it. It is advised not to exercise more than five times a week in the beginning. It is also recommended to start gradually and check the body's reaction over a period of time, and it is not recommended to exceed thirty minutes per day.

- Starting stretching exercises It is recommended to stretch before the main exercise, as this helps beginners to avoid injury, especially when doing the exercise for the first time. Stretching exercises should also be practiced after completing the sport so that each muscle is targeted separately for 15 seconds.







What is the best time to do sport ?
The best time to exercise is when a person can exercise continuously. There is no perfect and specific time to exercise, but there is an ideal time that suits each person in particular according to the nature of the day and lifestyle of these people.
